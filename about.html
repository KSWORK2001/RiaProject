Our mission · Community first

A platform built for dancers.

DANCETOPIA is here to help every dancer discover lessons, join classes, find events, explore festivals, and connect with people who move like you.

Discover & Learn

Find lessons and classes across styles and cities—beginner friendly, advanced technique, and everything between.

  • Curated classes and workshops
  • Studios and instructors in one place
  • Clear schedules and quick discovery

Showcase & Grow

Share your journey. Celebrate progress. Build a portfolio of performances and moments that matter.

  • Showcase posts and highlights
  • Follow dancers and crews
  • Grow through community feedback

Events & Festivals

Whether it’s a local jam or a multi-day festival, DANCETOPIA makes it easy to explore and plan together.

  • Events by style, location, date
  • Festival discovery and meetups
  • Save and share plans with friends

Connect like social media

Not just listings—connection. Chat, add people, and build real dance circles.

  • Profiles and friends
  • Messaging and group chats
  • Communities for every style
